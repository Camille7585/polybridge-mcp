# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

polybridge-mcp is an educational project. We are committed to making it a
welcoming space for students, teachers, junior developers, and experienced
engineers alike.

## Standards

Acceptable behavior :
- Asking questions, even basic ones
- Explaining things patiently to others
- Giving constructive feedback on pull requests
- Respecting that people come from different backgrounds and skill levels

Not acceptable :
- Condescending remarks toward beginners
- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing questions as "too basic"

## Enforcement

Report issues to convergence-tech@proton.me. Reports will be handled confidentially.
